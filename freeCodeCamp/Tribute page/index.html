The women who saved 1000K+ plants.

  • Saalumarada Thimmakka, also known as Aala Marada Thimmakka,
  • she is an Indian environmentalist from the state of Karnataka,
  • noted for her work in planting and tending to 385 banyan trees along a forty-five-kilometre stretch of highway between Hulikal and Kudur.
  • She has also planted nearly 8000 other trees. With the support of her husband, she found solace in planting trees
  • she has received Padma Shri award - 2019
